Crystals Collector!


-At the beginning of each game you will be given a random number.

-There are four crystals below that each have a different value that is hidden until you click it.
When you click on a crystal you will add an amount of points to your total score.

-To win the game you need to match your total score to the random number that is generated.

-Each time the game restarts the value of the crystals will change.
